Our Story

Sushi Yun has proudly served the City of Orange since 2009. As a woman-owned establishment, we take pride in offering a warm, welcoming atmosphere where every guest is treated like family. With always-available happy hour specials, signature rolls, and a wide selection of cooked options, there's something for everyone to enjoy.

We deeply value our regulars who make our continued success possible. If there's something special you'd like that's not on the menu, just ask our sushi chef — we'll be happy to make it for you.

Owner & Executive Chef

Passionate about Japanese cuisine

Meet Sophie

As the owner and executive chef of Sushi Yun, Sophie brings over 15 years of culinary expertise and an unwavering passion for authentic Japanese cuisine to every dish we serve.

Born in Korea and inspired by the precision and artistry of Japanese culinary traditions, Sophie moved to Orange County with a vision to create a space where quality and care come first. Her meticulous approach and commitment to sourcing only the finest cuts of fish have earned Sushi Yun a loyal following.
